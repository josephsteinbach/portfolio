Welcome to Authentic Jamaican

Jamaicans have been embodying the Natural, Local, & Slow Food philosophies for generations – without even trying! That’s why Pimento is so committed to product purity, seasonality, and mindful preparation. These are the cornerstones of our cuisine.

Family recipes meet French technique in all of our authentic Jamaican dishes.

Jerk Chicken

Marinated in our signature jerk rub, then fire grilled. (Choice of boneless or bone-in.)

Slow Roasted Jerk Pork

Marinated in our signature jerk spices, then cooked low and slow until fork tender.

One Love Special

A combination of fire-grilled jerk chicken and slow-roasted jerk pork.

Braised Oxtail

Cousin Pinky's original recipe- with butter beans, potato and country gravy.

Curry Goat

Slow cooked bone-in goat with curry, potato and island spices.

Curry Veggies

Seasonal veggies simmered in mild coconut curry and island spices. (Vegan)
